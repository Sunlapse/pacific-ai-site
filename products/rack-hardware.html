Where rack hardware fits.

Rack hardware supports the structure around the cooling loop. It helps align the CDU interface, rack manifold, in-rack routing, and server-level connections.

Layer 1

Rack Structure

Rails, mounting systems, brackets, and support hardware provide the mechanical foundation for high-density compute equipment.

Layer 2

Manifold Mounting

Rack hardware supports manifold positioning so supply and return paths align with server and coolant distribution requirements.

Layer 3

Hose Routing

Retention and protection components help keep hoses organized, protected, and serviceable inside dense rack environments.

Layer 4

Service Access

Mechanical layouts should allow upgrades, maintenance, and modular replacement without unnecessary disruption.
