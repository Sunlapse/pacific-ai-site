Quick Disconnect Couplings (QDC)

Representative connector types and configurations

Includes dry-break couplings, threaded connectors, valve-integrated fittings, and quick-release designs for liquid-cooled infrastructure.

High-reliability fluid connectors designed for liquid-cooled AI infrastructure. Quick disconnect couplings enable rapid connection and disconnection of coolant loops while minimizing fluid loss and supporting modular system deployment.

  • Dry-break, low-spill coupling design
  • Used in GPU cold plate, manifold, and CDU connections
  • Supports high flow rate liquid cooling systems
  • Available in multiple sizes and configurations (UQD, MQD, etc.)
  • Compatible with DI water and glycol-based coolant systems
  • Designed for serviceability and modular rack deployment
