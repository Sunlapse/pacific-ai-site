Hybrid Cooling Integration

Support for environments operating mixed air-cooled and liquid-cooled infrastructure. Pacific AI Infrastructure helps align components, rack-level cooling architecture, and deployment requirements for phased upgrades, partial liquid cooling adoption, and hybrid compute environments.

  • Supports mixed air-cooled and liquid-cooled rack environments
  • Useful for phased upgrades and partial rack conversion strategies
  • Aligns cold plates, manifolds, QDCs, CDU interfaces, and deployment hardware
  • Helps manage compatibility across existing and upgraded infrastructure
  • Supports expansion planning for growing GPU and accelerated compute density
  • Project-specific component alignment available based on cooling mix and deployment goals
