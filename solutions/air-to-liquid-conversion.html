Air-to-Liquid Conversion

Support for transitioning existing air-cooled infrastructure to liquid-cooled rack environments. Pacific AI Infrastructure helps align the cooling architecture, component selection, and deployment requirements needed for phased or full conversion programs.

What this supports

  • Supports retrofit planning for existing air-cooled environments
  • Aligns cold plates, manifolds, QDCs, CDU interfaces, and deployment components
  • Useful for higher-density GPU and accelerated compute rollouts
  • Supports phased upgrades as well as broader liquid cooling transitions
  • Helps reduce bottlenecks related to component compatibility and deployment coordination
  • Project-specific supply alignment available based on rack architecture and cooling approach

Workflow

1

Assess existing infrastructure

Review rack constraints, density targets, cooling limitations, and the current deployment environment.

2

Define cooling architecture

Align cold plates, manifolds, QDCs, CDU interfaces, and rack-level hardware to the target design approach.

3

Coordinate supply and deployment

Match sourced components to project requirements, deployment sequencing, and installation planning.

4

Support conversion execution

Help reduce friction during phased upgrades or full liquid cooling transition programs.
